Reading habit

I made a bet with a friend that if I missed one day of reading 25 pages, I'd pay him $25. The goal of this is to build the habit of reading everyday. Of course, I had to set some ground rules, like an EXCEPTED day where I can use once (1) a month within reason (exam season or I got in a car accident). What's so cool about this is that I get to see the progress that I get from reading the books I incessentaly purchase. This habit was inspired by Thomas Frank
